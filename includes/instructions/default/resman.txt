
<h1>Task Presentation</h1>
<p>In the resource management task, participants must limit the leakage from the two main tanks (A and B) by using a set of backup tanks and pumps, each with a specific power.</p>
<p>The participant's task is to turn on/off the numbered pumps to bring, as closely as possible, tanks A and B back to their normal levels.</p>
<p>Pumps may sometimes break down.</p>
